About LeEnat

LeEnat is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to supporting rural school girls in Ethiopia. Our goal is to ensure girls stay in school through menstrual hygiene support, education, dignity and empowerment.

🎯 Our Mission

📚 Education

Helping girls continue their education without interruption.

🩸 Hygiene Support

Providing menstrual hygiene products for rural school girls.

💜 Empowerment

Giving girls confidence, dignity and hope for the future.
